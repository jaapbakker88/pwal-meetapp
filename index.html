Why GoMeet?

We couldn’t find an simple or spontaneous way to connect with new people in real-life (that’s not dating). And it’s much easier to meet new people for a shared activity or event.

So we’re building that.

GoMeet is...

Meeting People for Activities & Events

An activity and event-based app: meet people doing stuff you like doing and at events you want to go to.

Simple & Spontaneous

Meet people today and do stuff together. Casual, free, no RSVP.

Community Driven

Add and join events (curated by the community) and meet 1-1 or in groups. Open-minded and fun-loving people need only apply.

GoMeet isn't...

A Dating App

There’s enough of those.

Swipe & Match-based

We think swiping and ‘matching for friends’ is kind of weird. Meeting for shared activities and at events is way less awkward.

Only Online

We’re more ‘connected’ digitally, yet more isolated, than at any time in history. Most social apps are only about online connections. We use technology to connect people online AND offline.

GoMeet for Sports

How it works?

Find people who want to play, or watch, the same sports as you:

  • Join a game of pick-up basketball, football or cricket
  • Watch the big game with fellow fans
  • Keep your running routine going, in a new city, with new running partners

GoMeet for Dining

How it works?

Find people into food and drink like you

  • Go to food festivals, cooking classes or farmers markets
  • Find fellow coffee, wine, craft beer or whisky aficionados
  • Organize dinner parties

MeetApp for Nightlife

How it works?

Find people to party with:

  • Go out to bars and club together
  • Join a group going to a music festival or club night
  • Promote your own party
